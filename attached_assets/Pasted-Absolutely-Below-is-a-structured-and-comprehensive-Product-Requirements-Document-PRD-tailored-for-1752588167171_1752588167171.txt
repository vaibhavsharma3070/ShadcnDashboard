1. Business Context
1.1 Business Overview
The company specializes in the resale of pre-owned luxury goods such as watches, handbags, and fashion accessories. Items are sourced from individual vendors who consign their goods with the company. The company markets these items, sells them to end clients, and takes a commission from each transaction.
1.2 Business Model Summary
Stakeholder	Role
Vendor	Supplies a luxury item and agrees on a fixed payout (consignment price).
Company	Markets, stores, sells the item, manages payments, and handles logistics.
Client	Purchases the item, either in full or through partial payments.
Flow:
1. Vendor consignment agreement is created (vendor price + listing price).
2. Item is stored and made available for sale.
3. A client pays in full or via instalments.
4. When fully paid, the item is delivered to the client and the vendor is paid.
5. Profit = listing price âˆ’ vendor payout âˆ’ item-related expenses.
1.3 Business Rules
* Items may require authentication or repairs, incurring expenses.
* Clients can pay in multiple instalments.
* Vendors are only paid once the item is fully paid by the client.
* Profit is calculated per item as:â€¨client payments - vendor payout - item expenses

2. Product Objective
Build a Retool-based internal web application that enables store operators, finance staff, and the business owner to:
* Register vendors and clients
* Log item intake and status
* Track client payments (partial or full)
* Register vendor payouts
* Record item-related expenses
* Monitor profitability in real-time

CREATE TABLE item (
    item_id               uuid            PRIMARY KEY DEFAULT uuid_generate_v4(),
    vendor_id             uuid            NOT NULL,
    title                 varchar(255),
    brand                 varchar(255),
    model                 varchar(255),
    serial_no             varchar(255) UNIQUE,
    condition             varchar(100),
    acquisition_date      date,
    agreed_vendor_payout  numeric(12,2)   CHECK (agreed_vendor_payout > 0),
    list_price            numeric(12,2)   CHECK (list_price > 0),
    status                varchar(20)     NOT NULL DEFAULT 'in-store', -- in-store | reserved | sold | returned
    created_at            timestamptz     NOT NULL DEFAULT now(),
    CONSTRAINT fk_item_vendor
        FOREIGN KEY (vendor_id) REFERENCES vendor(vendor_id)
        ON DELETE RESTRICT
);

CREATE INDEX idx_item_vendor  ON item(vendor_id);
CREATE INDEX idx_item_status  ON item(status);
